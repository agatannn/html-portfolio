Ride or Die movies according to Me, aka Agata

3 Best of the Best

Schindler's List (1993):

A powerful and moving film about Oskar Schindler's efforts to save Jewish lives during the Holocaust.

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly (1966):

A classic Spaghetti Western known for its epic scope and Ennio Morricone's iconic score.

Forrest Gump (1994):

A heartwarming and thought-provoking film that follows the life of an ordinary man through extraordinary historical events.
